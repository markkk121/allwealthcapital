FAQ

Do you have any Questions?

What is bitcoin ? +
Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency without a central bank or single administrator. It can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries. It was invented in 2008 by the pseudonymous Satoshi Nakamoto.
Who Developed Bitcoin? +
Bitcoin was developed by an unknown person or group of people using the name Satoshi Nakamoto. The original Bitcoin whitepaper was published in October 2008. The first Bitcoin software was released in January 2009.
What is Bitcoin Mining? +
Bitcoin mining is the process of creating new bitcoin by solving extremely complicated math problems that verify transactions in the currency. Miners receive Bitcoin as a reward for completing transaction blocks added to the blockchain.
Is Bitcoin Used For Illegal Activities? +
While Bitcoin has been used for some illegal activities, it is primarily used for legitimate purposes. The transparent nature of the blockchain actually makes it easier to track criminal activity compared to cash. The vast majority of Bitcoin transactions are for legal commerce and investment.

How fast are withdrawals processed? +
All Wealth Capital processes withdrawals instantly. Once you request a withdrawal, funds are sent directly to your wallet or bank account within 24 hours. We support bank transfers, crypto withdrawals, and multiple payment options.
